Our Services

At Quad Motors, we offer a comprehensive range of services to keep your luxury car in top condition. Our experienced technicians are trained to handle all makes and models, ensuring that your vehicle receives the best care possible. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we use state-of-the-art equipment and genuine parts to guarantee quality and reliability. Trust us to provide exceptional service and keep your car running smoothly.

Oil Change

Regular oil changes are essential to maintain engine performance and prolong the life of your vehicle.

Brake Service

Our brake service includes inspection, repair, and replacement of brake pads, rotors, and calipers.

Tire Rotation

Tire rotation helps ensure even wear and extends the life of your tires, improving safety and performance.

Engine Tune-Up

Our engine tune-up service includes inspection, cleaning, and adjustment of key components to improve performance.

Transmission

Our transmission overhaul service ensures smooth shifting and extends the life of your vehicle's transmission.

Paint Correction

Rust Protection

Our rust protection service helps prevent corrosion and extends the life of your vehicle.

Interior

Our interior detailing service cleans and restores the interior of your vehicle, leaving it looking and smelling like new.

Suspension

Our suspension tuning service optimizes your vehicle's handling and ride quality for a smoother driving experience.

Air-Con

Our air conditioning service ensures your vehicle's climate control system is working efficiently for your comfort.
