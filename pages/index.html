Welcome to Quad Motors!!!

Welcome to Quad Motors, which was established in 1988 as the place to find quality used vehicles in the Quad City area. Quadyster purchased the dealership in 2008, with Harsh at the helm. This family owned business is an alternative source for luxury cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs, and serves Quad, Davenport, Moline, Rock Island and surrounding communities. Quad Motors practices the same level of commitment as Quadyster with customer satisfaction always in mind. This established dealership stocks many different makes such as Ferrari, Lamborgini, AMG and strives to give fair prices on all vehicles. Please make sure you see Emily Johnson for all of your vehicle needs at Quad Motors. You can feel free to email, call, or simply stop in. Have a great day.
